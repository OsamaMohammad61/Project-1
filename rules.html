Wheel of Fortune - Rules

Welcome to the exciting world of Wheel of Fortune! Here's a breakdown of the basic rules to get you started:

Gameplay:
  • The game loads a random number in the fortune box.
  • Guess a consonant. If correct, you reveal the letter(s) in the puzzle and earn score based on the Fortune Box value (multiplied by the number of occurences of that letter in the word).
  • Vowels cost 25 of the fortune Box Value (multiplied by the number of occurences of that letter in the word) a
  • Solving the puzzle correctly wins you to the other word.
  • Six incorrect guesses will cause the game to be over
  • If you think you know the word, click on solve and take a risk, either get a huge score or loose all
